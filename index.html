VisiTrash was conceived on February 1-3rd of 2019 as a weekend Make-A-Thon project. Our mission was to reduce waste on an island resort by engaging with the public with visual rhetoric via the use of interactive designed trash receptacles.

Awareness of global warming, ocean plastic pollution and mass extinctions is growing worldwide as our window to contain the damage shrinks daily.
